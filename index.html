Where will the ceremony and reception be?

The ceremony and reception will take place at The Farm. When you arrive at 3:00pm you will be guided down to the macadamia orchard for the ceremony. This is a short walk - if walking isn’t ideal, just let us know, a car can be arranged to take you to and from the orchard.

Can I bring a date?

Please check your invite for a plus one.
